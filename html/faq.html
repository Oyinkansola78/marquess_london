How many fittings are required?

Two fitting sessions will be required to get the desired fit for your garment.

How long does the process take?
How long does the process take?

The act of craftsmanship requires a great deal of care at every stage. Generally, it takes no more than 7-8 weeks after the first fitting to have your new custom-made garment ready to take home.

What custom tailoring service do you offer?
What custom tailoring service do you offer?

We offer an exclusive Made-to-Measure service with the features of a bespoke service, with each client having a pattern block made exclusively for them. Our Made-to-Measure garments are constructed by machine with hand-made details for the final touch. We have two brand lines to offer: SWANK and Mod Con Line.

How do the Swank and Mod Con lines differ?
How do the Swank and Mod Con lines differ?

The Swank line uses both machine and hand-made construction, using branded clothes of the highest quality with wide-ranging cloth offerings from world-renowned merchants, which include Holland & Sherry, Ermenegildo Zegna, Reda, Vitale Barberis, Dugdale Bros, Guabello, Porter & Harding, Loro Piana and more. We offer a more extensive selection of style options, trimmings, and finishes. The Mod Con line is fully machine-made, using quality materials selected by us, with a range realised for developing style-appreciative gentlemen; relevant style choices to experience a custom commissionâ€¦"you dream it, we make it ".

Are second fittings included in the service?
Are second fittings included in the service?

Second fitting alterations are included in SWANK services, but all required alterations within ModCon are charged at a subsidised price, except length alterations, which are included as part of ModCon second fitting.

When do I need to book my second fitting?
When do I need to book my second fitting?

Second fittings should be done within 3 weeks of the earliest confirmed available date or extra charges could be incurred.
