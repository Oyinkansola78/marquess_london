2. STYLE & CLOTH SELECTION

With the discussion over and the information fresh on your mind, we jump into the selection process. This stage makes the garments truly yours, and includes choosing your desired fabrics, lining, and trimmings, through to the finer details such as lapel and pocket choices. We finish this stage by selecting the special elements of your garment, such as the font for any monogramming, side adjusters, trouser pleats, or jacket pick-stitching. These details are the finishing touches defining your unique, hand-crafted look and completing your Marquess experience.

3. INITIAL FITTING

Now you can relax and let us do our thing! Our experienced tailors will have all the information they need, and will gauge the correct balance between style, comfort, and body postures during your fitting. This is an interactive process, as we endeavour to understand your lifestyle and get to know how you wear the garment alike it to fit – this is something that can’t be achieved by simply one’s measurements. This stage should take normally take around 45 minutes and leads us to the end of your fitting.

4. FOLLOW-UP FITTING

Behind every masterpiece lies refinement. Regardless of the high quality, your garment comes back in after 4-6 weeks of production, our expert tailors will always review your garment on you to analyse its compatibility to your posture, back shape, and body movement. Adjustments will then be made to those small yet significant details, resulting in the optimal fit you dreamed of! Your Marquess journey has begun, and you will feel alive.

5. FINISHED PRODUCT

As a new member to the Marquess London family, we part with a Marquess handshake. Leaving you to embark on your continuous life journeys and our curated lifestyle events in the new investment added to your wardrobe. We see this as an investment, because unlike fast fashion, a custom-tailored garment in an impeccable cloth will last you years to come - presenting you with more value than you can thought possible.
